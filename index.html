About Us

Our core values

  • Innovation: Pioneering applications of location intelligence and spatial predictive analytics
  • Trust: Upholding rigor in security, privacy, ethics of data and models
  • Purpose: Driving positive impact through progress and empowerment enabled by our solutions

We provide transformative spatial intelligence solutions by combining the visualization power of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) with the predictive prowess of Machine Learning (ML) and the decision automation capabilities of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Our GIS mapping and analytics delivers precise location-based insights to reveal growth opportunities. Our ML algorithms enable complex geospatial and enterprise data modeling to drive accurate forecasts and predictions tuned to our clients' strategic needs and operational challenges. Furthermore, our AI expertise allows us to develop intelligent systems that optimize asset tracking, logistics planning, predictive maintenance and other processes through automated analysis of spatial patterns in near real-time data flows.
